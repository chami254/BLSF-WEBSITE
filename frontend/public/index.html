At blessed Leaning Foundation, we are on a mission to bring evalasting changes to the lives of vulnerable kids and families.
Our commitment is unwavering, ensuring that every support we provide is not just immediate but sustainable too.




Our Mission

To feed, cloth and foster street families.




Our Vision

To achieve an enviroment of love and respect for all.

BLSF runs several impactful programs designed to support the unique needs of people living with disabilities, with a special focus on children with cerebral palsy. Our services are designed to provide healing, growth, and lasting change.




Special Needs Education

Our learning programs are inclusive, adaptive, and focused on helping children develop intellectually and socially at their own pace. We work with special needs educators to ensure personalized support and care.

Medical Support & Monitoring

In partnership with healthcare providers, we ensure regular check-ups, access to medications, and health monitoring for children in our care. Our goal is to enhance health outcomes and promote stability in daily life.

Awareness & Advocacy

We are committed to breaking the stigma around disability. Through outreach campaigns, community events, and collaboration with local institutions, we promote inclusion, educate the public, and advocate for disability rights.

Education for All

We believe education is the foundation of a better future. Our program provides school supplies, scholarships, and mentorship to children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Food Security

Our food distribution initiative ensures that no child goes hungry. We partner with local farms and donors to deliver meals to underserved communities.

Mental Health Support

Mental health is often neglected in crisis. We offer therapy sessions, workshops, and community outreach to promote emotional well-being.
