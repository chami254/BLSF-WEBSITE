Who We Are

Blessed Leaning Shoulder Foundation (BLSF) is a grassroots non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the most vulnerable members of society. We believe that everyone deserves a shoulder to lean on in their time of need.

Our Mission

To uplift, empower, and provide care to marginalized communities through education, mental health support, and basic needs distribution.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to basic support systems and opportunities to thrive with dignity.

Our Values
  • Compassion
  • Equity
  • Integrity
  • Community
  • Empowerment
